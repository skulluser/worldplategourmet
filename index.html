Our Heritage

A Culinary
Journey Without Borders

Founded in 2009 by Executive Chef Éric Moreau, World Plate Gourmet was born from a singular vision: to bring the world's most extraordinary flavours to a single table. Each dish is a passport, each season a new destination.

Our kitchen team traverses continents, working hand-in-hand with artisan farmers, fishmongers, and purveyors to source ingredients of unparalleled quality. The result is a dining experience that transcends borders.

Éric Moreau

Executive Chef & Founder
