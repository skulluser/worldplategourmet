Our Heritage

The Beginning

Born of Wanderlust & Obsession

In 2009, Chef Éric Moreau returned to London after a decade of stages across Lyon, Tokyo, New York, and Marrakech with a singular conviction: the finest dining experiences on earth shared a common thread — absolute reverence for the ingredient.

World Plate Gourmet was conceived not as a restaurant with a cuisine, but as a living atlas of India's extraordinary gastronomic soul. The menu changes seasonally, the sourcing changes daily, and the curiosity never stops.

Fifteen years on, our kitchen team of twenty-two represents seventeen nationalities, six culinary traditions, and one shared mission: to place the world — beautifully — on your plate.

Journey Through Time

15 Years of Excellence

2009

Opening Night

World Plate Gourmet opens its doors in Mayfair to an intimate 40 covers. Opening night is sold out within hours.

2011

First Major Award

Named Best New Restaurant by the Evening Standard. Chef Moreau featured in TIME magazine's food issue.

2015

Wine Cellar & Expansion

A bespoke cellar of 800+ labels is installed. Private dining wing added with capacity for 40 guests.

2019

Michelin Recognition

Michelin recommends World Plate Gourmet in their Great Britain & Ireland Guide for five consecutive years.

2024

World's 50 Best Longlist

Featured on the World's 50 Best Restaurants extended list. James Beard Award nomination announced.

2025

15th Anniversary

Celebrating 15 extraordinary years. A special anniversary tasting menu and cellar release planned for autumn.

What Guides Us

Our Philosophy

Seasonality

Our menu breathes with the seasons. We follow nature's calendar, never forcing what the earth does not freely offer.

Provenance

Every ingredient is traceable to its origin. We visit our producers personally, building relationships built on trust and mutual respect.

Precision

Technique is the foundation upon which creativity is built. Our kitchen is a laboratory of refinement where perfection is the baseline.

Excellence

We hold ourselves to a standard that exceeds expectation — in every dish, every interaction, every element of the experience.
